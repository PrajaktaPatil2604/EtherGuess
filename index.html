Guess the Number Game
Welcome to the most fun and rewarding game! Guess the number and win Meme Coins! Ready to start?
Step 1: Claim Meme Coins
Get 100 Meme Coins to start the game. You'll use these coins to play.
Step 2: Make Your Guess
Guess the correct number between 1 and 100. Each guess costs 10 Meme Coins.
Step 3: Win Meme Coins!
If you guess correctly, you win 10 Meme Coins! Keep playing to improve your score.
